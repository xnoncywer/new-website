Our Presence in India

Our Growth Journey

Phase 1: Our Base - Mission IT Education (2018–2019)

  • The organization was founded with a vision to create meaningful industry impact and contribute to digital safety.
  • A dedicated core team of cybersecurity professionals was formed with a shared passion for protecting the digital ecosystem.
  • Strategic planning and groundwork were initiated to promote cybersecurity awareness and education.

Phase 2: Expansion and Collaborations (2020–2021)

  • Established collaborations with cyber awareness initiatives including Cyber Rakshak and Mission IT Education.
  • Launched cybersecurity awareness programs to strengthen digital literacy.
  • Engaged communities through educational initiatives and awareness campaigns.

Phase 3: Social Impact & Community Initiatives
(2022–2023)

  • Reached and engaged with 100+ communities across India through cyber awareness initiatives.
  • Earned recognition for contributions to social impact and national digital safety initiatives.
  • Delivered regular awareness drives, training sessions, and cybersecurity workshops.

Phase 4: Innovation, Collaboration & Growth
(2024–2026)

  • Proposed strategic partnerships with institutions and organizations through formal MoU agreements.
  • Emphasis on conducting cybersecurity awareness campaigns for educators and students, supported by specialized training and workshops.
  • Objective: To educate, secure, and empower individuals, institutions, and corporates against ongoing digital threats.
